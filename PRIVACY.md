# Privacy Policy — My Boss Is an AI

**Last updated: July 17, 2026**

**My Boss Is an AI** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

- **No accounts.** The game has no sign-up, login, or user profiles.
- **No tracking.** There are no analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers of any kind.
- **No network use.** The game works entirely offline. Your progress is saved only on your device.
- **Game Center (optional).** If you are signed in to Apple's Game Center, the game can report your achievements and leaderboard scores through Apple's GameKit framework. This data is handled entirely by Apple under [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). The game itself never sees, stores, or transmits your Game Center identity.
