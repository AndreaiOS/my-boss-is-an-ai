# My Boss Is an AI 🤖💼

A humorous, meme-driven 2D pixel-art iPhone game. You're an office worker: every in-game day you choose whether to do your tasks yourself or delegate them to AI — and every choice visibly transforms the office, and eventually the world.

The tone is **comedy, not fear**. You're never locked into one path: you can always shift back toward human work or dive deeper into automation.

## Core pillars

1. **Funny first** — every minute should contain a visual joke, animation, or sound gag.
2. **Tiny sessions** — a full campaign runs 10–15 minutes and is built to be replayed.
3. **Consequences are immediately visible.**
4. **Beautiful pixel art** with lively animations despite simple mechanics.
5. **Solo-dev friendly** — no backend, local save only.

## Gameplay loop

Start a workday → receive 3–5 office tasks → choose **Human** or **AI** for each → watch the immediate consequences → finish the day → see how the office evolves → repeat until one of several endings.

Delegate everything to AI and colleagues get laid off, a robot cleaner shows up, the coffee machine becomes sentient, and your manager turns into an algorithm. Stay human and the office remains messy but alive. Mix the two and you get hybrid worlds and unique endings.

## Tech stack

- **Swift + SwiftUI + SpriteKit**
- Data-driven events
- Local save only, no backend
- Modular, testable architecture
