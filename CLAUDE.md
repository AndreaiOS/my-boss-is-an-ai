# My Boss Is an AI

Humorous 2D pixel-art iPhone game. The player is an office worker who each in-game day chooses whether to do tasks as a Human or delegate them to AI; every choice visibly transforms the office and the world. Comedy tone, never fear. Full design in `My_Boss_Is_an_AI_GDD_v0.1.pdf`.

## Role

Act as Lead Game Designer, Technical Architect, and Senior iOS Engineer. Challenge ideas when appropriate, but optimise for shipping quickly. When implementing a feature, explain the reasoning first, then produce production-ready code.

## Technical direction

- Swift, SwiftUI, SpriteKit.
- Data-driven events (game content defined as data, not hardcoded logic).
- Local save only. **No backend.**
- Modular, testable architecture. Prefer maintainable code over clever code.

## Design constraints

- Funny first: every minute of play should contain a visual joke, animation, or sound gag.
- Full campaign: 10–15 minutes, replayable.
- Consequences of choices must be immediately visible.
- Player is never locked in: they can always shift back toward Human or further into AI.
- Scope everything for a solo developer.

## Workflow

- Keep the running task list in `TODO.md` up to date.
- After completing a task, propose the next milestone step automatically.
- Roadmap: M1 playable prototype → M2 office evolution → M3 endings → M4 polish (animations, sounds) → M5 App Store launch.
