**Description** (4000):

 You work in an office. Your boss just discovered AI. Good luck.

 MY BOSS IS AN AI is a bite-sized comedy about work, robots and one very important mug. Every day you get a handful of office tasks — reply to 47 emails, fix the printer (again), sign Karen's birthday card — and one choice for each: do it yourself, or let the AI handle it.

 Every choice visibly changes your office. Delegate everything and watch your colleagues get "optimized", a robot vacuum start judging your crumbs, and your manager become a pie chart with opinions. Stay human and the office remains messy, loud and wonderfully alive. Nothing is permanent: rehire Gino, bring back the barista, resurrect the meme wall.

 FEATURES
 • A full campaign in 10–15 minutes, built to be replayed
 • 7 endings, from the Corporate Singularity to Employee of the Century — collect them all
 • Meeting duels: out-wit angry clients armed with corporate jargon
 • A consultant in a plaid suit who will absolutely not stop knocking
 • The AI itself comments on your choices (it keeps logs)
 • Tap anything in the office — everything has an opinion
 • Handmade pixel art, chiptune sound gags, zero menus between you and the fun
 • Game Center achievements and leaderboard

 NO ads. NO in-app purchases. NO data collection. NO internet required. The only artificial intelligence here is the one stealing your job.
